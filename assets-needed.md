## Asset Preparation Tips

### Photo Guidelines
- Natural lighting preferred
- Simple, non-distracting background
- High resolution (can always downsize)
- Avoid heavy filters

### Image Optimization
Before uploading images:
1. Resize to maximum needed size
2. Compress using [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com) or [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app)
3. Use WebP format where possible (Framer/Webflow handle this)

### File Naming
Use consistent, descriptive names:
- `facilitator-photo.jpg`
- `og-image.png`
- `favicon-32.png`
- `testimonial-sarah.jpg`
