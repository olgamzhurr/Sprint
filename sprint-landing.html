I am your Sprint Partner

I started this sprint because I love experimenting - and I'm a team player. I move faster and think better when I'm not doing things alone.

For years, I've led Data and AI initiatives in corporate environments, building solutions that drove hundreds of millions of dollars in business value. At some point, I asked myself: if I can do this for big companies, can I do the same for a small project of my own - just following the same methodology that corporations use to build new products?

That's how this sprint was born.

I'm inviting you to work with me in the same room for 2–3 hours a day, over 10 days within one month. We'll work side by side, going through practical exercises together - defining which idea to pursue, thinking through a financial model, and using AI tools to create images, websites, and so much more.

This isn't a course. It's focused time with examples, facilitation, and protected space to take the right steps toward building a business - doing the work together, but each on our own project.
