Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't have a clear idea yet?
Perfect. Day 1 is specifically about discovering and validating ideas. Many participants start with nothing more than "I want to build something" and leave with a launched business. The process helps you focus.
How much time do I need?
Plan for 2-3 hours per day for the live sprint sessions, plus some independent work time. It's intensive by design-that's how we create momentum. If you're doing the self-paced workbook, you control your schedule.
What if I can't make all the live sessions?
Sessions are recorded and shared with participants. But honestly? The magic is in doing it live together. The accountability, real-time feedback, and energy of the group are hard to replicate async.
Is this for digital or physical products?
Both. The framework works for apps, SaaS, services, physical products, and more. We've had participants launch everything from coaching businesses to e-commerce stores to software products.
What happens after the 10 days?
You'll have a launched business-that's the goal. But you'll also join our alumni community where past participants support each other, share wins, and continue building together.
